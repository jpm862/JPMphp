Biography

Looking for someone who can get things done? A problem solver where no challenge is too big? I enjoy smoothing out the wrinkles technology and people bring. I’m a Technology Professional who has done it all – software development, DevOps, installation and deployment, enterprise design and customer relations. This wealth of experience allows me to step in and contribute wherever needed.

As a Developer, I have worked extensively with distributed, multi-tiered systems crossing multiple languages and technologies. Agile, Unit Tests and TDD were standard. I applied my experience in operations to establish automated build, deployment and test processes – before DevOps was a term.

As an Analyst, I focused on building strong relationships with business, management, developers and support staff. I have extensive experience gathering business requirements, turning them into technical specifications and translating that into a vision for others to follow.

As an Integrator, I know how all of the pieces and parts work. Software, operating systems, hardware, networks – I know how they interconnect, what troubleshooting tools to use and when to dig for more information.

As a Technical Interpreter, I understand problems from every side – the Business Unit, Developers or IT staff. Establishing the requirements and concerns of each group, I then explain them to the other teams to obtain compromises to complex problems.
